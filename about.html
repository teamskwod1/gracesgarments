About Me

Hello and welcome!


My name is Grace Gordon (she/her) and I'm from Winston-Salem, North Carolina. I graduated from
Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, New York with a major in Fashion Design and minor in Sustainable Studies.


My love for fashion is nonexistent without the presence of sustainability-driven design. There is no fashion
for me if it is at the expense of another, no matter how big or small—because people come before profits.
Alongside my technical skills, I am continuing to develop my knoweldge of sustainable and ethical practices.
As I feel a growing sense of urgency about the state of our climate, our fellow humans, and our planet, I am
committed to sustainability that encompasses every aspect of the supply chain.


I have always been concerned that we will find ourselves telling our children stories of coral reefs, forests, and
glaciers, the same way our parents tell us stories of landlines and Rolladexes. Why can't the beauty of fashion
coexist with the beauty of clean ocean waters, lush rainforests, flourishing ecosystems, and breathable air?
